Pyramid Formulas

Volume: $$ {V =\frac{1}{3}\times \text{Luas Alas}\times \text{Tinggi}} $$

Luas Area: $$ {A = s^{2} + {4} \times (\frac{1}{2} \times(s) \times \text{t_segitiga})} $$

Example

$$ {\text{anggap }s = 10 \text { dan } t = 30} $$

$$ {V = \frac{1}{3}\times{10}^{2}\times{30} = {1000}} $$

$$ {{A} = {10}^2+{4}\times(\frac{1}{2}\times{10}\times{30}) = 700} $$
